What It Does
This is tool is designed to help understand what is stochatistc programming. It focusses on spatial explicit stochastic programming where the decision variables are binary. This tool tries to display the solution for a forest management planning under climate change uncertainty.

Under climate change it is assumed that forest growth and yield will be different from the historical data. However, different climate change forcast lead to different results and therefore a particular attention is needed from forest managers in order to take the best decision that will remain optimal regardless of which future climate under consideration materializes in the next future
